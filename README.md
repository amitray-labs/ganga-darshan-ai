## 🌏 Vision

**Ganga Darshan AI** is the main umbrella project of the **Sri Amit Ray
Compassionate AI for Ganga Mission (CAGM)** — an open-source initiative
that harnesses the power of Compassionate Artificial Intelligence to
monitor, protect, and rejuvenate the sacred **River Ganga** (Ganges).

The Ganga flows over **2,525 km** across India, sustaining over
**500 million people**, countless ecosystems, and millennia of
spiritual heritage. Today, she faces unprecedented threats from
industrial pollution, sewage discharge, agricultural runoff, plastic
waste, microbial contamination, and climate change.

**Our mission:** Deploy AI not as a cold technological tool, but as a
**compassionate digital guardian** — one that respects the river's
sacredness, empowers local communities, ensures ecological justice,
and drives measurable environmental restoration.

## 🏛️ Project Architecture

Ganga Darshan AI is organized as a **modular, interconnected ecosystem**
of specialized repositories, each focusing on a critical dimension of
river health monitoring and restoration.
