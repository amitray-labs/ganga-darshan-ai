## 🌏 Vision

**Ganga Darshan AI** is the main umbrella project of the **Sri Amit Ray
Compassionate AI for Ganga Mission (CAGM)** — an open-source initiative
that harnesses the power of Compassionate Artificial Intelligence to
monitor, protect, and rejuvenate the sacred **River Ganga** (Ganges).

The Ganga flows over **2,525 km** across India, sustaining over
**500 million people**, countless ecosystems, and millennia of
spiritual heritage. Today, she faces unprecedented threats from
industrial pollution, sewage discharge, agricultural runoff, plastic
waste, microbial contamination, and climate change.

**Our mission:** Deploy AI not as a cold technological tool, but as a
**compassionate digital guardian** — one that respects the river's
sacredness, empowers local communities, ensures ecological justice,
and drives measurable environmental restoration.

## 🔬 The 12 Compassionate AI Principles for Ganga

Inspired by **Sri Amit Ray's Compassionate AI Framework**, every
component of this project adheres to these guiding principles:

1. **Ahimsa (Non-Harm)** — AI shall cause no harm to the river,
   its ecosystems, or the communities that depend on it.
2. **Karuna (Compassion)** — Every algorithmic decision is rooted
   in compassion for all living beings in the Ganga ecosystem.
3. **Satya (Truthfulness)** — Data integrity, transparent
   reporting, and honest uncertainty quantification.
4. **Seva (Selfless Service)** — Technology serves the river and
   communities, not corporate or extractive interests.
5. **Dharma (Ecological Duty)** — Upholding the ecological and
   spiritual dharma of the river.
6. **Interconnectedness** — Recognizing that water quality,
   biodiversity, human health, and spiritual well-being are
   inseparable.
7. **Local Empowerment** — AI augments (never replaces) local
   knowledge, community monitoring, and traditional practices.
8. **Precautionary Intelligence** — When uncertainty exists,
   AI errs on the side of ecological protection.
9. **Equitable Access** — All tools, data, and insights are
   freely available to all stakeholders.
10. **Intergenerational Justice** — Decisions consider impacts
    on future generations.
11. **Adaptive Learning** — Systems continuously learn and
    improve while remaining humble about limitations.
12. **Sacred Respect** — Honoring the Ganga not merely as a
    water body but as a living, sacred entity.

## 🚀 Quick Start

### Prerequisites

- Python 3.9 or higher
- Git
- Conda or virtualenv (recommended)
- GPU with CUDA support (optional, for deep learning modules)

### Clone All Repositories

```bash
# Clone the umbrella repo
git clone https://github.com/CAGM/ganga-darshan-ai.git
cd ganga-darshan-ai

# Initialize and clone all sub-project repositories
./scripts/clone_all_subprojects.sh
